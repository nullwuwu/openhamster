# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep collaboration technical, respectful, and reviewable.

Examples of expected behavior:
- discuss behavior, evidence, and tradeoffs directly
- assume good intent while challenging weak assumptions
- keep reviews specific and actionable
- document operational and safety implications clearly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or publishing private information
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- bad-faith reviews or hostile gatekeeping

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related community spaces managed for OpenHamster.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participants whose behavior harms collaboration.
